About TOKUZO JUDO ACADEMY

By learning and practicing Judo, you will become stronger and also develop courage, politeness, self-control, sincerity, consideration, respect, and helpfulness.

Tokuzo Judo Academy develops people who are helpful and necessary in society through the rigorous practice of Judo.

Tokuzo Takahashi

Tokuzo started his judo carrier at Koyosha Dojo in Hokkaido at the age of 7. He is a graduate of Tenri High School and Tenri University, where he was a member of Tenri Judo. Prior to assuming his post at Los Angeles Tenri Dojo, he was a member of the Nippon Steel Judo Team. While at Nippon Steel, he was selected as a Senior Level National Team trainee. He took part in the national team training camps and represented Japan in international competitions. Also, he was a winning member of the Nippon Steel Team when they won the All-Japan Judo Team Championships in 2000. Also, he won some medals in the All-Japan Corporate Championships and some international tournaments in the weight divisions of -90kg and -100kg. His superb judo skills have been demonstrated in his competition records in the U.S. He is an eight-time champion in the open division at the U.S. Senior Nationals and a two-time World Champion in the IJF Veterans Championship.Â
